3. Third-Party Platform Integrations

GenerArise integrates with the following third-party platforms to deliver its services. Below is a detailed explanation of each integration:

🎵 TikTok — Login Kit: We use TikTok's Login Kit to securely authenticate your TikTok account. This allows third-party users (you) to connect your own TikTok profile to GenerArise so that you can manage and publish your own content. We only request access to your basic profile information (display name and avatar). You can revoke this access at any time from your TikTok account settings under Security → Manage app permissions.

📤 TikTok — Content Posting API (Share Kit): We provide a tool for independent users to use TikTok's Content Posting API to upload and publish their own short-form video content directly to their own TikTok accounts. The content is created by our AI engine based on your preferences and niche. GenerArise is a multi-tenant SaaS platform, and this integration is strictly for our third-party users to manage their presence, not for internal company use or personal use by the GenerArise team. You maintain full ownership and editorial control over published content.

📸 Meta — Instagram & Facebook Graph API: We use Meta's Graph API to publish images, Reels, and text posts to your connected Instagram Business Account and Facebook Page. This requires a valid Meta access token that you authorize during the connection process.

📺 Google — YouTube Data API v3: We use the YouTube Data API to upload YouTube Shorts to your channel. OAuth 2.0 is used for secure authentication, and you can revoke access at any time from your Google Account security settings.
