Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026

1. About GenerArise

GenerArise is a multi-tenant SaaS (Software as a Service) AI-powered content automation and social media management platform designed for independent third-party businesses and creators. Our application enables these third-party users to automatically generate, schedule, and publish their own content across multiple social media platforms including TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, and YouTube using their own authenticated accounts. We do not use these integrations for internal company operations or personal use by our team. GenerArise also provides AI-driven lead discovery, scoring, and automated sales outreach tools.

2. Information We Collect

When you use GenerArise, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, and password when you create an account.
  • Social Media Tokens: OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens provided by third-party platforms (TikTok, Meta, Google/YouTube) when you authorize our application to publish content on your behalf.
  • Content Data: Text, images, and videos generated by our AI engine for publication on your connected social media accounts.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our platform, including pages visited and features used.
  • Business Data: If you use our Lead Marketplace or Sales Engine, we may process business contact information sourced from publicly available directories.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our services.
  • To generate and publish content on your authorized social media accounts.
  • To authenticate your identity and manage your account.
  • To send you notifications about your content schedule, publishing status, and account activity.
  • To provide customer support.

4. Third-Party Platform Integrations

GenerArise integrates with the following third-party platforms. Below is a detailed explanation of each integration and the data scopes we request:

🎵 TikTok Integration

Login Kit: We use TikTok's Login Kit to authenticate your TikTok account. This allows independent users (you) to securely connect your own TikTok profile to the GenerArise platform to manage your own content. We only request access to your basic profile information (display name and avatar).

📤 TikTok Content Posting API

Content Posting (Share Kit): We provide a tool for independent users to use TikTok's Content Posting API to upload and publish their own AI-generated video content directly to their own TikTok accounts. This integration is strictly for our third-party SaaS users to manage their presence, not for internal company use or personal use by the GenerArise team. You maintain full control and can revoke access at any time from your TikTok security settings.

📸 Meta (Instagram & Facebook)

We use Meta's Graph API to publish images, videos (Reels), and text posts to your connected Instagram and Facebook accounts. Access tokens are stored securely and used exclusively for authorized content publishing.

📺 YouTube

We use the YouTube Data API v3 to upload short-form videos (YouTube Shorts) to your channel. OAuth tokens are securely stored and refreshed automatically.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties. We only share data with trusted third-party services that assist us in operating our platform (such as cloud hosting providers and social media APIs), and these parties agree to keep this information confidential.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. Social media access tokens are encrypted at rest and transmitted over secure HTTPS connections. We regularly review our data collection, storage, and processing practices.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our services. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time.
  • Revoke access to any connected social media platform from the respective platform's security settings (TikTok, Facebook, Google).
  • Request a copy of your data or its complete deletion.
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications.

9. Cookies

We use essential cookies to maintain your session and preferences. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of significant changes via email or in-app notification.
